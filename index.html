Hi, I'm Adhem.

I’m a C# developer using the Asp.Net Core framework, a college student, and a world traveler. I have plenty of work experience for my age, however, I want to create experiences and see the people who use my software light up with joy. Although C# is my most proficient language, I also have experience creating games in Unity 2D/3D, creating static websites in HTML/CSS, and have created a full stack app using C#/Asp.Net Core, JS/Angular, and MSSQL. My dream is to program video games that inspire individuals. To start, I’m working on getting my foot in the door as a backend/full-stack programmer with internships and small-scale projects.
